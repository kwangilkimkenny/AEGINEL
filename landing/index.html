How it works

PII protection in three steps. Zero configuration needed.

1

You type normally

Use ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any supported AI service as usual. Aegis Personal monitors your input silently in the background.

2

PII is detected and masked

Before your message is sent, Aegis detects personal information and replaces it with realistic pseudonyms. SSNs become different SSNs, emails become different emails.

3

AI responds, you see the original

The AI works with pseudonymized data. When it responds, Aegis automatically restores your real data in the chat display — seamless and invisible.
