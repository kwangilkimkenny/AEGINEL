## Detailed Description

**AEGINEL — Don't ban AI. Use it safely.**

AEGINEL is a privacy-first AI safety extension that protects you while using ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and 25+ other AI services. It runs entirely in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

### Key Features

**Privacy Protection (PII Proxy)**
- Automatically detects and masks personal information before it's sent to AI
- Supports: Social Security numbers, credit cards, emails, phone numbers, passport numbers, Korean RRN (주민등록번호)
- Pseudonymized data is sent instead — AI responses are automatically de-masked back

**9-Layer Threat Detection**
- Prompt injection & jailbreak detection
- Data extraction attempt blocking
- Social engineering pattern recognition
- Korean/CJK script evasion detection
- Encoding attack detection (Base64, ROT13, etc.)
- Multi-turn attack signal analysis
- ML-powered classification (on-device ONNX model)

**Silent by Default**
- No annoying popups for safe conversations
- Small shield icon shows protection status
- Full alerts only when real threats are detected
- Weekly protection reports show your safety stats

### Supported Sites (25+)
ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, Perplexity, Mistral, DeepSeek, Grok, Poe, HuggingChat, Character.AI, Meta AI, Google AI Studio, GitHub Copilot, and more.

### Privacy First
- 100% local processing — no data sent to external servers
- No account required
- No tracking or analytics
- Open source

### Permissions Explained
- **storage**: Save your settings and scan history locally
- **activeTab**: Detect which AI site you're on
- **offscreen**: Run ML model for threat detection
